## 一、选型类

### Q1：风冷机和水冷机怎么选？

**客户可能问法：**
- "Air-cooled or water-cooled - which is better for us?"
- "你们风冷和水冷哪个好？"
- "什么时候选水冷？"

**标准答案（中文）：**
选择取决于皮带宽度和应用场景：
- **风冷机**：皮带宽度 300-1800mm，设备轻便、操作简单、维护成本低，适合大多数通用场景
- **水冷机**：皮带宽度 1200mm 以上，处理更宽更厚的皮带，加热均匀、效率高，适合重载矿山、港口等严苛场景

简单判断：如果皮带宽度超过 1200mm，或者应用在高温、高负荷环境，选水冷。其他情况选风冷。

**标准答案（英文）：**
> "It depends on your belt width and application:
>
> **Air-cooled presses** are ideal for belts up to 1,800mm width. They're portable, easy to operate, and lower maintenance. Most general belt maintenance shops use air-cooled.
>
> **Water-cooled presses** are designed for belts 1,200mm+ and heavy-duty applications - mining, ports, steel mills. They offer more uniform heating and faster cycle times for thick, large-area splices.
>
> What's your typical belt width? And what's the application - maintenance shop or field work?"

**补充说明：** 如果客户说两种都用，可以推荐组合方案——风冷做日常维护，水冷做大活。

### Q14：操作安全注意事项？

**客户可能问法：**
- "What safety precautions?"
- "操作有什么危险？"

**标准答案（中文）：**
主要安全要点：
- 高温部件（加热板）：操作时避免直接接触，加热板温度可达 200°C
- 高压：保压阶段有压力，避免在高压状态下调整夹具
- 电气：三相电操作需接地，电工安装
- 防护装备：操作时建议戴隔热手套

**标准答案（英文）：**
> "Safety guidelines:
>
> - **Hot surfaces**: Heating platen reaches up to 200°C. Heat-resistant gloves required during operation.
> - **High pressure**: Do not adjust fixtures while under pressure.
> - **Electrical**: Three-phase power requires proper grounding by a certified electrician.
> - **Training**: Operators should complete basic safety training before first use.
>
> We provide full safety documentation with each machine."
