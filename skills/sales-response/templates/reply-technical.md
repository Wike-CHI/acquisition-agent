# 技术问题回复模板

> 适用场景：客户询问具体技术参数、选型建议、兼容性问题。

---

## 使用原则

1. **先确认** - 客户的技术问题有时是基于不完整信息的假设，先确认再回答
2. **给选择** - 技术问题往往有多种方案，给出选项让客户判断
3. **留后续** - 留一个后续跟进的问题或建议

---

## 选型确认

```
Subject: Re: [原邮件主题] - Model Selection

Hi [姓名],

Thank you for your technical question. Before I give you a recommendation, I need to confirm a few details:

1. Maximum belt width you work with: [?]mm
2. Typical belt thickness: [?]mm
3. Belt material: Rubber / PVC / PU / Steel cord / Other [?]
4. Application: Maintenance shop / Field work / [?]
5. Frequency of use: Daily / Weekly / [?] splices per month

With these details, I can recommend the exact model and configuration that fits your operation. A model that's too small won't work, and one that's too large is an unnecessary expense.

What's your typical setup?

Best regards,
Wike Chen
```

```

Hi [姓名],

Regarding [belt material type] compatibility:

YES, our equipment handles [material]:
- Standard configuration: [temp]°C, [pressure] Bar
- Standard silicone pads rated for this material
- Typical splice quality: [result]

For your belt specs:
- Width: [X]mm → Model [X] recommended
- Thickness: [Y]mm → Within standard range
- Material: [Z] → Standard configuration works

If your application has any special requirements (high-temperature environment, food-grade belt, etc.), let me know and I'll confirm the configuration.

Wike Chen
```

## 技术跟进问题清单

每次回答技术问题后，可以追加以下问题之一：

- "What's the main challenge you're trying to solve right now?"
- "Is this for a current project or future planning?"
- "Do you have a target budget for this investment?"
- "What's your timeline - is this urgent or more exploratory?"
- "Would it help to see some photos of your current setup?"
