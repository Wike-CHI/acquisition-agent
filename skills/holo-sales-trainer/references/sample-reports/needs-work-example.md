## 原始话术

> Hi Carlos,
>
> Thanks for your interest in our products. We are a professional manufacturer of industrial belt processing equipment with 20 years of experience. Our products include air-cooled machines, laboratory core cutting machines, guide bar machines, and more.
>
> Regarding your inquiry, we can supply the AC-1200 air-cooled machine and the LC-800 laboratory core cutting machine. The AC-1200 uses a German Siemens compressor and the LC-800 has a cutting width of 800mm.
>
> Our prices are competitive and we can deliver within 30 days. We also have CE and ISO certifications.
>
> Please let me know if you need more information.
>
> Best regards,
> Wike

## 关键教训

这封邮件犯了B2B外贸最常见的错误：**把开发信写成产品手册目录**。好的开场是问问题而不是陈述事实。记住这个公式：

> **开场 = 你了解我什么 + 我能帮你解决什么**
> 
> 而不是
> 
> **开场 = 我们是XX公司 + 我们有XX产品 + 请联系我们**
