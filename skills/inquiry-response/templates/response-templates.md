# 邮件回复模板

> 询盘应答的常用邮件结构模板

## 模板1：回复技术问题

```
Subject: RE: [original subject] — Technical Details

Hi [Name],

Thanks for your question about [topic].

[Direct answer, 3-5 sentences max. Use bullet points if listing specs.]

[If applicable: "I've attached [document] with more details."]

Quick question for you — [one relevant question to keep conversation going,
e.g., "what belt type are you currently working with?"]

This will help me recommend the best configuration for your setup.

Best regards,
Wike Chen
Sale Manager | HOLO Industrial Equipment Mfg Co., Ltd
+86 131 6586 2311 | wikeye2025@163.com
www.beltsplicepress.com
```

## 模板4：跟进邮件（Day 3-7）

```

Wike Chen
```

## 模板5：推动成交（强信号时）

```
Subject: RE: [original subject] — Next steps

Hi [Name],

Based on our conversation, it sounds like the [model] would be a good fit
for your [application].

Here's what the next steps look like:
1. I prepare a formal quotation / proforma invoice
2. You review and confirm specifications
3. 30% deposit to initiate production
4. [X] working days to complete production
5. Photos/video of finished product for your approval
6. Balance payment + shipment

If you'd like to move forward, I just need:
- [Your company full name and address]
- [Confirm shipping terms and destination]
- [Any final spec changes]

Shall I prepare the quotation?

Best regards,
Wike Chen
```

## 模板6：最终跟进（Day 30）

```
Subject: Checking in / Closing the loop

Hi [Name],

I've reached out a few times and I don't want to be a pest.

I'm guessing the timing isn't right, which is totally fine. I'll close
your file for now.

If things change down the road — new equipment needs, budget opens up,
or you just want to chat about conveyor belt splicing — my door is always
open.

All the best with your current projects.

Wike Chen
```
