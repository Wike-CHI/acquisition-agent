# 兴趣信号应答

> 当客户表现出购买意向时，快速推进成交
> 识别信号 + 推进策略 + 话术模板
> 版本：1.0.0 | 更新：2026-04-11

---

## 什么是兴趣信号？

客户说的不是"我要买"，而是通过各种方式暗示他在认真考虑。
识别这些信号并及时推进，是把线索变成订单的关键。

---

## 信号识别

### 强信号（马上要买的迹象）

| 信号 | 客户原话示例 | 意味着 |
|------|-------------|--------|
| 问价格细节 | "What's your best price for the 1200mm model?" | 在做预算 |
| 问交期 | "How soon can you ship?" | 有急单需求 |
| 问付款方式 | "What payment terms do you offer?" | 在做采购计划 |
| 问技术细节 | "Can it handle 15mm rubber belt?" | 在做技术评估 |
| 要正式报价 | "Can you send me a formal quotation?" | 要提交审批 |
| 提到现有设备问题 | "Our current press keeps overheating" | 有痛点要解决 |
| 问售后 | "What if something breaks?" | 在评估风险 |
| 问物流 | "How much is shipping to Santos port?" | 在算总成本 |
| 要测试/样品 | "Can I see a test video?" | 要验证质量 |
| 指定规格 | "I need 220V 60Hz version" | 在做配置决策 |

### 中等信号（有兴趣但还在比较）

| 信号 | 客户原话示例 | 意味着 |
|------|-------------|--------|
| 问对比 | "How does this compare to Flexco?" | 在做竞品比较 |
| 问定制 | "Can you customize the voltage?" | 在匹配需求 |
| 问MOQ | "Do you have minimum order?" | 在评估门槛 |
| 要目录 | "Send me your full catalog" | 在做调研 |
| 问OEM | "Can you put our logo on it?" | 考虑经销 |
| 提到项目 | "We have a project coming up" | 有潜在需求 |
| 问保修 | "How long is the warranty?" | 在评估风险 |

### 弱信号（值得关注但不急）

| 信号 | 客户原话示例 | 意味着 |
|------|-------------|--------|
| 只说thanks | "Thanks for the info" | 礼貌性回复 |
| 说未来 | "Maybe next year" | 暂时没需求 |
| 说会联系 | "We'll be in touch" | 委婉拖延 |
| 只关注邮件本身 | "How did you get my email?" | 冷启动客户 |

## 推进策略

### 强信号 → 直接推进

客户给了强信号，不要犹豫，直接推下一步。

**问价格 → 给价格 + 推进**
```
EN:
For the [model], the price is $[X] FOB Ningbo.

This includes:
- Equipment with [voltage] configuration
- Standard accessories and spare parts kit
- Export wooden packaging
- 12-month warranty

To move forward, I can prepare a formal quotation for your review.
I'll need:
1. Your company name and address (for the quotation)
2. Your preferred shipping terms (FOB/CIF/EXW)
3. Destination port or city

Shall I prepare that now?
```

**问交期 → 锁定时效**
```
EN:
Current lead time for [model] is [X] working days from deposit confirmation.

However, our production schedule is filling up for [month]. If you can
confirm within this week, I can lock in the [current month] production slot
and guarantee shipment by [date].

After that, we're looking at [next month] production at the earliest.

Would you like me to reserve a slot while we finalize the details?
```

**要正式报价 → 加速准备**
```
EN:
Absolutely — let me prepare that right away.

To make the quotation accurate, please confirm:
1. Model(s) and quantity
2. Belt specifications you'll be working with (type/thickness/width)
3. Voltage and frequency requirements
4. Shipping terms (FOB/CIF/EXW) and destination
5. Any special requirements

I'll send the formal quotation within 24 hours with pricing valid for 30 days.
```

### 中等信号 → 提供信息 + 软推进

客户在比较和调研，提供信息帮他们做决策，同时轻轻推一下。

**问对比 → 帮他比较**
```
EN:
Great question. Here's an honest comparison:

| Feature | HOLO | Flexco | Typical Chinese |
|---------|------|--------|-----------------|
| Price | $$ | $$$$ | $ |
| Quality | European standard | Top tier | Variable |
| Lead time | 15-20 days | 60-90 days | 20-30 days |
| Warranty | 12 months | 12 months | 6-12 months |
| Custom specs | Yes, fast | Limited | Yes |
| Support | Lifetime remote | Regional | Limited |

HOLO sits in the sweet spot: European-grade quality at a competitive price,
with fast delivery and flexible customization.

Want me to send a detailed spec sheet for the model you're considering?
```

**问定制 → 确认需求**
```
EN:
Yes, we customize regularly. Let me understand your requirements:

1. What voltage and frequency do you need? (e.g., 220V/60Hz, 380V/50Hz)
2. Any specific belt width beyond our standard range?
3. Special temperature requirements?
4. Any preferences for control system or interface language?
5. Physical size constraints in your workshop?

Once I have these details, I can confirm feasibility and lead time.
Most customizations add 10-15 working days to standard lead time.
```

**问OEM → 这是一个大机会**
```
EN:
Yes, we offer full OEM/ODM services. This is actually one of our strengths —
several brands in the industry use HOLO as their manufacturing partner.

What we can customize:
- Branding (logo, colors, company name)
- Packaging and documentation
- Machine specifications and features
- Control panel interface and language

I'd love to learn more about your distribution plans. Are you looking to:
a) Brand our existing models under your name?
b) Co-develop a custom product line?
c) Something else?

Let's schedule a call to discuss details.
```

### 弱信号 → 保持温度

不要急，保持联系频率，提供价值。

**只说thanks → 7天后跟进**
```
Day 0: No further reply needed. Wait.
Day 7: Send something useful — not salesy.
  "Hi [Name], I came across this article about [topic relevant to their
   industry] and thought you might find it interesting. No reply needed."
Day 14: Light check-in.
  "Hi [Name], just checking if you had a chance to review the catalog
   I sent. Happy to answer any questions whenever the timing is right."
Day 30: Final follow-up.
  "Hi [Name], I don't want to clutter your inbox. Should I close your
   file for now, or is this still on your radar? Either way is fine."
```

**说未来 → 锁定时间**
```
EN:
Understood — timing is everything. Let me ask:

Is there a specific timeframe you're looking at? (next quarter? next year?)

If so, I can:
1. Set a reminder to follow up at the right time
2. Send you periodic updates on new models or pricing changes
3. Prepare a budget estimate for your planning

No commitment needed. I just want to be helpful when the time is right.

When would be a good time to reconnect?
```

## 快速推进技巧

### 技巧1：假设成交

不要问"你要不要买"，而是假设他已经在买，讨论细节：

```
不说: "Would you like to place an order?"
而说: "When we ship this to you, do you prefer Ningbo or Shanghai port?"
```

### 技巧2：二选一

给两个选项，无论选哪个都是往前走：

```
不说: "What do you think?"
而说: "Would the air-cooled or water-cooled model work better for your application?"
```

### 技巧3：限时激励

制造合理的不紧迫感（不是虚假的）：

```
EN:
Just a heads up — we're increasing prices by ~5% starting [date] due to
raw material costs. If we can finalize before then, I can lock in the
current pricing for you.
```

### 技巧4：消除最后一个顾虑

```
EN:
It sounds like this would be a good fit. Is there anything else holding
you back? If it's [common concern], here's how we handle that...

If everything looks good, shall I prepare the proforma invoice?
```
