## 一、风冷机 FAQ

### Q01: 风冷机和水冷机有什么区别？怎么选？

**短回答：** 皮带厚度≤10mm选风冷，>10mm选水冷。

**详细回答(EN):**
```
The choice depends on your belt thickness and material:

**Air-Cooled Press** — for belts ≤10mm thick
- Lighter, more portable
- Faster setup (no water supply needed)
- Ideal for PVC, PU, thin rubber belts
- Models: 300mm to 3600mm

**Water-Cooled Press** — for belts >10mm thick
- More stable temperature control
- Better for thick rubber conveyor belts
- Required for heavy-duty mining/cement applications
- Models: 600mm to 4200mm

If you tell me your belt type, thickness, and width, I can recommend
the exact model.
```

## 二、水冷机 FAQ

### Q07: 水冷机需要接自来水吗？

**回答:**
```
Not necessarily. Our water-cooled presses use a closed-loop water circulation
system:

- Built-in water tank (no need for continuous water supply)
- You fill it once and the system recirculates
- Just top up occasionally as water evaporates
- Can use regular tap water (distilled is better but not required)

For field operations, you can use a portable water container.
```

### Q08: 水冷机的冷却方式是怎样的？

**回答:**
```
Our water-cooled presses use aluminum cooling plates:

1. Heating phase: silicone heating plates bring the joint to 140-160°C
2. Pressure phase: pneumatic system applies uniform pressure
3. Cooling phase: water circulates through aluminum cooling plates,
   rapidly dropping the temperature while maintaining pressure
4. Release: once temperature drops to safe level, pressure releases

The water-cooling phase ensures the joint sets properly without warping.
This is critical for thick rubber belts (>10mm).
```

### Q09: 水冷机最大能处理多宽的皮带？

**回答:**

Our largest water-cooled press handles belts up to 4200mm wide.

Full range:

- 2100, 2600mm (standard duty)
- 3200, 3600, 4200mm (heavy duty, well-type frame)

For belts wider than 4200mm, we can discuss custom solutions.

## 三、分层机 FAQ

### Q10: 分层机是用来做什么的？

**回答:**
```
A ply separator splits a multi-ply rubber conveyor belt into individual layers:

Main applications:
1. **Belt repair** — separate the cover rubber from the carcass to patch
   damaged areas without replacing the entire belt
2. **Belt preparation** — before splicing, you need clean layer edges
   for proper adhesion
3. **Material recovery** — separate usable materials from old belts

Without a ply separator, this is done manually with knives — slow,
inconsistent, and potentially dangerous.

HOLO offers European-style and American-style ply separators in
130mm and 380mm widths.
```

### Q11: 欧式分层机和美式有什么区别？

**回答:**
```
Both do the same job, but the design approach differs:

**European-style (A1FCJ):**
- Horizontal cutting action
- Better for standard belt widths (up to 380mm)
- Precise depth adjustment
- Available in single-phase 220V and three-phase 380V

**American-style (A2FCJ):**
- Vertical cutting action
- Handles wider belts more easily
- 130mm model available
- Single-phase 220V or three-phase 380V

For most customers, we recommend the European style for its precision.
For heavy mining belts, the American style may be more practical.
```

## 五、打齿机 FAQ

### Q13: 为什么需要打齿？

**回答:**
```
Tooth-cutting (finger punching) creates interlocking finger profiles at
belt ends before splicing:

Why it matters:
1. **Stronger joints** — the finger-over-finger design distributes stress
   across the entire joint width
2. **Better adhesion** — increased surface area for bonding
3. **Flexibility** — finger joints flex more naturally than straight cuts,
   reducing stress on the splice

For high-tension belts (mining, steel cord), tooth-cutting is essential.
For light-duty PVC/PU belts, a straight cut may be sufficient.

HOLO offers manual, pneumatic, and automatic tooth-cutting machines
from 300mm to 4000mm.
```

### Q14: 手动打齿和自动打齿怎么选？

**回答:**
```
**Manual (A1DCJ)** — for low volume or occasional use
- Lower cost
- Suitable for smaller operations
- Available in 300-1500mm
- Requires more operator skill

**Pneumatic (B1DCJ)** — for medium volume
- Faster and more consistent than manual
- Air-powered cutting action
- 1100mm head / 2600mm body

**Automatic (C2DCJ)** — for production environments
- Motorized feed and cutting
- Most consistent tooth profiles
- Highest throughput
- Available in 1000-4000mm

Rule of thumb: if you do more than 10 joints per week, go pneumatic
or automatic.
```

## 八、选型引导 FAQ

### Q21: 客户只说"我需要一台硫化机"怎么推荐？

**回答流程：**
```
Ask these questions in order:

1. "What type of belt do you work with?" (PVC/PU/Rubber/Other)
   → Determines air-cooled vs water-cooled

2. "What's the belt thickness?" (mm)
   → ≤10mm = air-cooled, >10mm = water-cooled

3. "What's the belt width?" (mm)
   → Determines the model size

4. "What's your local power supply?" (voltage/frequency)
   → Determines electrical configuration

5. "Is this for workshop use or field service?"
   → Affects portability and stand requirements

Based on the answers, recommend the specific model and configuration.
```

### Q22: 客户不确定自己的皮带规格怎么办？

**回答:**
```
No problem — here's how to figure it out:

1. **Belt type**: Look at the belt surface — smooth = PVC/PU, textured
   with fabric pattern = rubber. If it has fabric edges showing, it's
   likely PVC.

2. **Thickness**: Use a caliper or ruler at the belt edge. If you don't
   have one, compare with a coin: a US quarter is 1.75mm thick.

3. **Width**: Measure the full width of the belt on the conveyor.

4. **Application**: What industry are you in? This helps us narrow down
   the belt type even without exact measurements.

Photos work great too — send me a picture of the belt edge and I can
estimate the specifications.
```

## 九、售后技术 FAQ

### Q23: 加热板不加热了怎么办？

**回答:**
```
Troubleshooting steps:

1. Check power supply — is the machine plugged in and switched on?
2. Check the temperature controller — is it set to the correct temperature?
3. Check the solid-state relay (SSR) — indicator light should be on
   during heating
4. Check the thermal fuse — may have tripped if machine overheated
5. Check heating element continuity with a multimeter

Most common cause: thermal fuse trip (easy fix, spare included in kit)

If the issue persists, send us a photo of the controller display and
we'll diagnose remotely. Or schedule a video call for live troubleshooting.
```

### Q24: 温度控制不准怎么办？

**回答:**
```
Possible causes and solutions:

1. **Thermocouple loose** — reseat the thermocouple in the heating plate
2. **PID needs recalibration** — we can guide you through recalibration
   via video call
3. **Heating element aging** — replace if unit is 3+ years old
4. **Airflow obstruction** — for air-cooled units, check that vents
   are clear

Temperature accuracy should be ±1°C with our Eurotherm controller.
If you're seeing ±5°C or more, something needs attention.

Contact us with your model number and we'll provide specific guidance.
```

### Q25: 压力不够怎么办？

**回答:**
```
Check these items:

1. **Air supply pressure** — verify input pressure is 0.6-0.8 MPa
2. **Thomas pump** — check that the pump is running and building pressure
3. **Airbag condition** — inspect for leaks (listen for hissing)
4. **SMC pressure gauge** — verify the reading matches set pressure
5. **Seals and connections** — check all pneumatic fittings for leaks

The airbag (from Jiangsu Baojinli) is rated for 160°C continuous use
and typically lasts 2-3 years.

If the airbag is leaking, we can ship a replacement — they're designed
to be user-replaceable.
```
