## 一、付款方式

### Q01: 你们接受什么付款方式？

**回答:**
```
We offer several payment methods:

Standard terms:
- T/T (Telegraph Transfer / Bank Wire): 30% deposit, 70% before shipment
- T/T 100% in advance (for small orders under $3,000)

For established customers (after 3+ successful orders):
- T/T 30% deposit + 70% against copy of B/L
- Net 30 days (subject to credit review)

We accept payments in USD and EUR. For other currencies, please ask.

Note: We do not currently accept L/C (Letter of Credit) for orders
under $10,000 due to bank processing fees.
```

### Q02: 能不能用信用证(L/C)付款？

**回答:**
```
Yes, we accept irrevocable Letters of Credit for orders over $10,000.

Requirements:
- Irrevocable, at sight
- Issued by a top-tier bank
- Valid for 90 days from issue date

L/C processing typically adds 7-10 days to the order timeline.

For orders under $10,000, we recommend T/T as the L/C bank fees
(usually $200-500) make it less cost-effective.
```

### Q03: 可以分期付款吗？

**回答:**
```
For new customers, our standard terms are 30% deposit + 70% before shipment.

However, we can discuss flexible arrangements for:
- Large orders (over $20,000)
- Government or institutional purchases (with purchase order)
- Established customers with good payment history

Please share your specific situation and I'll see what we can arrange.
```

## 二、价格与报价

### Q04: 报价包含什么？不包含什么？

**回答:**
```
Our quotation includes:
✅ Equipment (as specified)
✅ Standard accessories and tools
✅ Operation manual (English)
✅ Wooden case / export packaging
✅ Factory testing and quality inspection

Not included (unless specified):
❌ International shipping / freight
❌ Customs duties and import taxes
❌ Local delivery from port to your facility
❌ Installation (remote guidance is free; on-site installation available at extra cost)
❌ Insurance during transit (can be arranged at cost)

All prices are FOB Ningbo/Shanghai unless otherwise stated.
```

### Q05: 你们的价格有效期多久？

**回答:**
```
Our quotations are valid for 30 days from the date of issue.

After 30 days, prices may be adjusted due to:
- Raw material cost changes
- Exchange rate fluctuations
- Component price changes from suppliers (Thomas pumps, SMC, etc.)

If you need an extended validity period (60 or 90 days), please let me
know and I'll note it on the quotation.
```

### Q06: 可以打折吗？

**回答:**
```
Our pricing already reflects competitive factory-direct rates. However,
we offer volume discounts:

- 3-5 units: 3-5% discount
- 6-10 units: 5-8% discount
- 10+ units: 8-12% discount (case by case)

Other ways to save:
- Combine multiple items in one shipment (save on freight)
- Order during our promotion periods (ask about current offers)
- Annual framework agreement (locked pricing for 12 months)

Let me know your requirements and I'll provide the best possible price.
```

## 三、交货与物流

### Q07: 交货期多久？

**回答:**
```
Lead times depend on the model and customization:

In-stock standard models:
- Air-cooled 600-1200mm: 5-7 working days
- Common water-cooled models: 7-10 working days

Standard production (no customization):
- 15-20 working days

Custom specifications:
- 30-45 working days

Shipping from China:
- To Southeast Asia: 7-12 days by sea
- To Middle East: 18-25 days by sea
- To South America: 30-40 days by sea
- To Europe: 25-35 days by sea
- To Australia: 15-20 days by sea

Urgent? We can arrange air freight at additional cost (5-7 days worldwide).
```

### Q08: 可以用客户指定的货代吗？

**回答:**
```
Absolutely. We're flexible on shipping arrangements:

Option 1: We arrange shipping (FOB Ningbo/Shanghai)
- We handle Chinese-side logistics and export customs
- You handle freight and import clearance

Option 2: You arrange shipping (EXW / FOB)
- We deliver to your designated freight forwarder in China
- You coordinate all logistics

Option 3: Door-to-door (CIF / DAP)
- We handle everything to your facility
- Higher cost but zero hassle for you

Just let me know your preference and we'll adjust the quotation accordingly.
```

### Q09: 包装是什么样的？海运安全吗？

```
Export packaging standards:

1. Machine wrapped in anti-rust VCI film

This packaging is tested for ocean freight conditions and we've shipped

For extra-sensitive electronics, we can add a wooden pallet base for
```

## 四、质保与售后

### Q10: 质保期多久？保什么？

**回答:**
```
Standard warranty: 12 months from date of delivery

Covered:
- Manufacturing defects
- Component failures under normal use
- Electrical/electronic failures (controllers, relays, sensors)
- Structural defects

Not covered:
- Consumable parts (heating elements, airbags, seals)
- Damage from misuse, modification, or negligence
- Normal wear and tear
- Damage during transit (covered by shipping insurance)

How to make a warranty claim:
1. Send photos/video of the issue
2. Our engineer diagnoses remotely (usually within 24 hours)
3. We ship replacement parts by express (free under warranty)
4. We guide you through replacement via video call

In 2025, our warranty claim rate was under 1%.
```

### Q11: 质保期过了怎么办？

**回答:**
```
After the warranty period:

- Lifetime remote technical support (free)
  - WhatsApp/Email/Video call troubleshooting
  - No charge for technical advice

- Spare parts at factory cost
  - We keep stock of all common wear parts
  - Typically ship within 3 days
  - No markup — you pay what we pay

- Paid on-site service available
  - For complex issues that can't be resolved remotely
  - Engineer dispatch to your facility (travel costs apply)

Most customers never need on-site service. Our remote support resolves
95%+ of issues.
```

### Q12: 有备用配件清单吗？

**回答:**
```
Yes. Each machine comes with a recommended spare parts list:

Essential spares (included with machine):
- Extra heating element (1 set)
- Fuses and thermal cut-offs
- Common seals and gaskets
- Allen keys and basic tools

Recommended spare parts kit (optional, $150-300 depending on model):
- Additional heating elements

- Airbag (spare)

- Complete set of fuses

We recommend ordering a spare parts kit with your initial purchase
to minimize any future downtime.
```

## 五、文件与合规

### Q13: 能提供什么出口文件？

**回答:**
```
Standard export documents included:
- Commercial Invoice
- Packing List
- Bill of Lading (B/L)
- Certificate of Origin (CO)
- CE Certificate (copy)

Available on request:
- Form A / Form E / Form F (for preferential tariff rates)
- Material Safety Data Sheet (MSDS) — not typically needed for our equipment
- Phytosanitary certificate (for wooden packaging)
- Pre-shipment inspection report (SGS, BV, etc.)

Special certificates for specific markets:
- SONCAP (Nigeria)
- PVOC (Kenya, Tanzania)
- SASO (Saudi Arabia)

Please inform us of your country's import requirements when placing the order.
```

### Q14: 需要进口许可证吗？

**回答:**
```
This depends on your country. Our equipment (HS code 8477) generally
falls under "industrial machinery" category:

Common situations:
- Most countries: no special import license needed
- Some countries require: pre-shipment inspection or certification
- Restricted in very few markets

We recommend checking with your local customs broker. If you let me
know your country, I can share what previous customers in your region
have experienced.
```
