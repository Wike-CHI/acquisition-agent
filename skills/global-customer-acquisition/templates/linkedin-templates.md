# LinkedIn Templates

## Connection Request

```
Hi [Name],

I noticed you're the [Title] at [Company]. We manufacture [Product Type] and have been serving companies like [Similar Company] in [Industry].

Would you be open to connecting?
```

```
Hi [Name],

Thanks for connecting! I noticed [Company]'s work in [Industry]. 

We manufacture [Product Type] and I thought there might be an opportunity to support your operations.

Would you be open to a brief chat about your [Related Needs]?
```

## Follow-up Message

```
Hi [Name],

Just following up on my previous message. Any interest in learning more about our [Product]?

Happy to share more details or answer any questions.
```
