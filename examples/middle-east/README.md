- Iran

## Communication Guidelines
- **Language**: English for B2B communication (Arabic welcome but not required)
- **Tone**: Formal, relationship-first
- **Greetings**: Use honorifics; "Mr." / "Eng." preferred
- **Pacing**: Do not rush decisions — Middle Eastern partners value trust-building
- **Follow-up**: 3–5 days after initial WhatsApp message

## Market Notes
- Saudi/UAE: Major infrastructure projects driving conveyor belt demand
- Iran: Import restrictions make reliable suppliers valuable; focus on documentation/packaging compliance
- Decision-makers often senior (owner, GM, procurement director)
- WhatsApp messages should be concise; save detailed proposals for email
